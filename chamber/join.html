Membership Levels

NP Membership (Non‑Profit)

No fee for qualifying non‑profit organizations. Support, networking, and event access.

Bronze Membership

Basic membership benefits including directory listing and event invitations.

Silver Membership

Enhanced visibility, promotional opportunities, and priority event access.

Gold Membership

Premium benefits with featured placement, sponsorship options, and premium support.

NP Membership (Non‑Profit)

Benefits:

  • No membership fee for qualifying organizations
  • Access to networking events and volunteer opportunities
  • Listing in the business directory
  • Discounted sponsorship rates for community events

Bronze Membership

Benefits:

  • Directory listing and basic profile
  • Invitations to member events
  • Access to member-only resources
  • Opportunities to join committees

Silver Membership

Benefits:

  • Enhanced directory placement and profile highlights
  • Promotional opportunities (social posts, newsletters)
  • Priority access to event registrations
  • Discounts on chamber services

Gold Membership

Benefits:

  • Featured placement on website and printed materials
  • Sponsorship opportunities for major events
  • Dedicated support and promotional consulting
  • Exclusive networking with chamber leaders
