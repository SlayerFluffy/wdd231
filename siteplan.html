Site Name

Cedar Rapids Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This site is a resource for the Cedar Rapids, Iowa Chamber of Commerce. It will include information about the community, weather, calsl to action and business spotlights. It will also allow users to discover locale history, demographics, and current events. It will provide an image montage of visitor attractions. It will guide visitors to local business and organizations within the chamber of commerce. It will also give the opportunity for users to submit a member application to join the chamber of commerce.

Target Market

Business owners and visitors to Cedar Rapids, Iowa.
