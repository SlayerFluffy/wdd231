About The Pawfect Match

The Pawfect Match began with a simple idea — that every pet deserves a loving home and every person deserves the joy of a loyal companion. Founded in 2012 by a small group of animal lovers, our shelter started in a single renovated barn with just a handful of rescue animals. Over the years, we’ve grown into a community-driven adoption center that connects hundreds of cats and dogs with families every year. Through compassion, care, and a belief in second chances, The Pawfect Match has become a place where wagging tails and happy hearts find their way home.

Our Mission & Values

At The Pawfect Match, our mission is to bring people and pets together through kindness, respect, and love. We believe that every adoption is more than a rescue — it’s the start of a lifelong friendship. Our team works tirelessly to provide safe shelter, proper medical care, and gentle socialization for every animal in our care. We value compassion, community, and commitment — not just to the animals who need us, but to the people who open their hearts to them. Together, we’re creating a world where every paw has a place to call home.
